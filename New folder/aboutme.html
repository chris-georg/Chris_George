About


    Hi! I'm Chris George, a passionate web developer specializing in creating responsive, user-friendly websites that bring your business and brand's vision to life. With expertise in front-end development, I help businesses enchance their online presence through innovative, efficient web solutions.


    My journey into web development started with a curiosity for how websites were built, and it quickly became a full-time passion. With a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I enjoy transforming ideas into interactive digital experiences.

When I’m not coding, you might find me exploring the latest in design trends, reading about web accessibility, or trying my hand at creating pixel art. I also enjoy a good coffee break and believe that great ideas often come when you least expect them! Let’s Connect!


My Skills & Toolbox


I specialize in front-end technologies and am always excited to explore new tools that help create seamless user interfaces. Here are some of the main technologies I work with:
  • Languages & Frameworks: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and TypeScript
  • Styling: SCSS, Tailwind CSS, Bootstrap
  • Tools: VS Code, Git, GitHub, Figma, Adobe XD

My Approach


    I believe in designing for the user. My goal is to ensure that every site I build isn’t just functional, but also feels intuitive and visually appealing. I'm committed to writing clean, maintainable code and keeping up with best practices in accessibility and web performance.
